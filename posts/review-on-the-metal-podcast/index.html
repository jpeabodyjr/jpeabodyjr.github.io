Review: 'On the Metal' Podcast

February 28, 2020 1-minute read

Historically, I’m not a regular listener of podcasts. It’s usually audiobooks for me during the daily commute. Recently though, my colleague Sebastian recommended “On the Metal: Tales for the Hardware/Software Interface”. I was blown away!

Each episode features a new guest who describes various tales from the hardware/software interface. The interview format kept the conversation moving resulting in an extremely entertaining, mind opening listening experience. Then I realized the show notes provide links to all the topics I wanted to research from each episode; mind blown.

A huge shout out to hosts Jessie Frazelle and Bryan Cantrill for wrapping up the first season!

I’m looking forward to season two.
